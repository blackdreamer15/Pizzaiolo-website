ABOUT US

Pizzaiolo is an heritage that has been passed by through generations by great Pizaiolos. It is the creation of Baki Jessy Justice Julien commonly known as Jessy Julien, the greatest black african Pizzaiolo in history.
A Pizaiolo, we hold at heart our core values are we go by them in our everyday work and make our pizzas with the deepest part of our heart with love because we believe around a pizza, love is dissipated and a pizza brings together people of all walks of life.

Quality

Because at Pizaiolo, we wants to dissipate love through well-made pizzas, we put our best in the work we do. We have a special team well-trained to monitor every single activity undertaken and the finished products from the ovens.

Satisfaction

At Pizaiolo, a satisfied customer is a ball of joy thrown in our garden. We treat our customers and clients with high-esteem and provide them with quality services in hygienic conditions to make them feel just at home.

Availability

For our customers and clients, we have decided to make ourselves available 24h/7 to see a smile on their faces and for them to enjoy their pizzas anytime of the day. Because everyone deserves a pizza whenever s/he wants.
